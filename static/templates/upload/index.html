Video CMS

Video CMS Prototype

For best viewing use chrome

This is a Python Django Web App to allow our clients to drag and drop files on a web page to upload to our broadcast system. This would have became the basis for our video content management system. In contrast, the client had to use a cumbersome FTP program prior to the web app. This made the process of uploading video and images into our computer system smoother and easier.

/master

Open this route in another webpage that you can view at the same time as the /form. This route is where an admin or master control person could view all of the video and image content coming in from all of the clients. It allowed them to preview, comment and accept. When they accept the content and auto email is sent out.

/form

This route would be for the client. They add all there information into the form and submit. Then they are taken to the drag and drop upload section.
